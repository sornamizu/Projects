Welcome!

This is a simple HTML and CSS-based study buddy website to help you stay organized and motivated. Use it as a quick reference for study techniques, tips, and a basic to-do list. For timers or dynamic features, pair it with your phone or browser tools.

Study Tips

  • Set specific goals for each study session (e.g., "Read Chapter 3").
  • Take short breaks to avoid burnout—try the Pomodoro Technique below.
  • Study in a quiet, distraction-free environment.
  • Review notes actively by summarizing in your own words.
  • Stay hydrated and get enough sleep for better focus.

Pomodoro Technique

A simple time management method: Study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. After 4 cycles, take a longer 15-30 minute break. Use a timer app on your phone since this site is static.

  • 25 min: Focused work
  • 5 min: Break (stretch, walk)
  • Repeat 4x, then long break
